Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose

1897-1945

Leader of Indian National Army

Subhas Chandra Bose (23 January 1897 – presumed dead on 18 August 1945) was a prominent Indian nationalist whose defiant patriotism made him a hero in India. He was one of the most prominent leaders of the Indian independence movement against British colonial rule. He famously gave the slogan "Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom!" He sought international support for India's independence during World War II and formed alliances with Axis powers to fight the British. His life and mysterious disappearance remain topics of much interest and debate in India.
